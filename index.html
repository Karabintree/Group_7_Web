About Us

Welcome to YYC Puppies, Calgary's premier destination for professional puppy grooming since 2004. With a deep passion for pets and years of experience, we provide top-notch grooming services that prioritize both the comfort and well-being of your furry companions.

Our team of dedicated groomers understands the unique needs of puppies, offering gentle handling and personalized care in a calm and nurturing environment. From basic grooming to specialized treatments, we ensure that each puppy receives the attention they deserve.

Join us at YYC Puppies and discover why pet owners across Calgary trust us for exceptional grooming services. We look forward to pampering your puppy and becoming your trusted partner in their care.
